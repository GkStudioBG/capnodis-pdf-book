## PART 1: FUNNEL STAGES & REEL CONCEPTS

### Funnel Overview
```
AWARENESS (Reel 1) → Problem is real, dry trees, "why?"

DECISION (Reel 3) → Here is the solution, here's what you get, buy now
```

## REEL 1 — AWARENESS: "The Invisible Enemy"

**Concept:** A single dry almond tree in a Spanish orchard. The farmer walking around sees the leaves yellowing and thinks "drought" — but the real problem is underground.

**Hook line:** "Si se ve como sequía pero el riego ha sido suficiente... no es sequía."

**Visual concept:**
- Wide shot of sun-scorched Spanish almond orchard (extreme rural Spain, Almería/Murcia aesthetic)
- Camera pushes into ONE tree — the most stressed one
- Cut to: farmer's hand brushing soil away from trunk base, revealing small holes and frass
- Cut to: dark close-up of larva in root cross-section (animated diagram or real macro)
- End: Text "El daño empieza bajo tierra" — dark background, gold text

**Key emotion:** Confusion → Recognition (aha moment)

**CTA:** Watch more / Save post

---

## REEL 2 — CONSIDERATION: "Why Your Nematodes Failed"

**Concept:** Many farmers tried nematodes. They didn't work. They blame the product — but the timing, soil moisture, and application were wrong. This reel speaks to the farmer who already tried something and it failed.

**Hook line:** "¿Aplicaste nematodos y no funcionaron? No siempre fallan ellos."

**Visual concept:**
- Split screen: sad/failed nematode application vs. successful one
- Animated infographic: "Humedad del suelo >40%" / "Temperatura 15-30°C" / "Aplicar en larvas activas"
- Quick cuts: soil moisture meter, temperature reading, calendar pointing to right month
- Show the PDF guide section on nematodes as the solution
- End: "La guía te explica qué revisar antes de comprar"

**Key emotion:** Relief + validation ("it wasn't just me")

**CTA:** Save post / Send to farmer friend

---

## REEL 3 — DECISION: "19,90€ — Less Than One Treatment"

**Concept:** Compare the price to wasted treatments. €19,90 is less than one nematode treatment that might fail. Or less than one consultation with an agronomist. Frame as investment protection.

**Hook line:** "19,90 € — Menos que un tratamiento que puede fallar."

**Visual concept:**
- Fast montage of wasted products / failed treatments / money going out
- Side-by-side: ❌ €80 nematodes + failed vs. ✅ €19,90 guide + system
- Show the 3 bonuses: calendar, checklist, decision tree
- End on: "Acceso anticipado: 19,90 € + 3 bonus imprimibles — Comprar ahora"

**Key emotion:** Rational decision — this is smarter than guessing

**CTA:** Buy now

## PART 2: ADDITIONAL CREATIVE ANGLES (Brainstormed)

### ANGLE 4 — SEASONAL: "Before Summer, Get Your Calendar"

**Headline:** "Antes de que llegue el calor, ten tu calendario."
**Sub-headline:** "Los adultos empiezan a salir con el calor. Necesitas saber cuándo observar y cuándo actuar."
**Visual:** Calendar page flipping, showing April-May action items, overlaid on orchard in spring bloom
**CTA:** "Ver calendario — 19,90 €"
**Funnel:** Awareness → Decision (seasonal urgency)

**Why it works:** Spanish farmers think in seasons. April-May is the start of adult activity — the ad hits when the pain is top of mind and action is still possible.

**Compliance note:** Don't say "before it's too late" (too urgent/manipulative). Say "before the season starts" (practical).

---

### ANGLE 5 — PREVENTION: "New Orchard? Start Here"

**Headline:** "¿Plantando almendros? Este error sale caro."
**Sub-headline:** "El gusano cabezudo puede llegar sin que lo veas. Mejor prevenir que reaccionar."
**Visual:** Young almond plantation with soil cross-section showing roots, protective barrier mesh visible, farmer consulting a tablet
**CTA:** "Guía + prevención: 19,90 €"
**Funnel:** Awareness (new planters, less urgent audience)

**Why it works:** Captures forward-thinking farmers who haven't had the problem yet. Lower pain, higher willingness to pay for prevention. This audience is easier to convert — they're already in planning mode.

**Compliance note:** Don't claim the guide prevents 100%. Say "reduce el riesgo" and "ayuda a detectar temprano".

---

### ANGLE 6 — COST vs. LOSS: "What Is One Tree Worth?"

**Headline:** "Un solo árbol muerto puede costar más que esta guía."
**Sub-headline:** "Un almendro adulto representa €200-€500 en producción. ¿Cuántos tienes en riesgo?"
**Visual:** Single mature almond tree with yellow leaves, crossfade to price calculation overlay: "1 tratamiento fallido: €80", "1 árbol muerto: €300+", "Guía completa: €19,90"
**CTA:** "Proteger la inversión — 19,90 €"
**Funnel:** Decision (cost-justification)

**Why it works:** Price anchoring against loss, not against product cost. Makes the €19,90 feel cheap compared to the potential loss.

**Compliance note:** Don't give specific yield calculations unless verified. Use ranges and "hasta" framing.

---

### ANGLE 7 — PEER / COMMUNITY: "Other Farmers Ask About This"

**Headline:** "Otros agricultores me preguntan: ¿cómo saber si es gusano cabezudo?"
**Sub-headline:** "He preparado una guía para productores de almendro que quieren entender el problema y actuar con más criterio."
**Visual:** POV from farmer in field, looking at damaged tree, phone showing guide PDF, voiceover style or text-to-video
**CTA:** "Ver la guía — 19,90 €"
**Funnel:** Consideration (social proof / peer recommendation)

**Why it works:** Farmers trust other farmers. This angle positions the product as peer-vetted. It feels less like an ad and more like a recommendation from a colleague.

**Compliance note:** Don't use fake farmer testimonials. This is the creator speaking, not fabricating quotes.

---

### ANGLE 8 — INTEGRATED PEST MANAGEMENT (IPM): "Science-Based, Not Hype"

**Headline:** "No es magia. Es manejo integrado."
**Sub-headline:** "La guía no promete eliminar la plaga. Te ayuda a entender el ciclo, detectar señales y actuar con un plan basado en fuentes técnicas."
**Visual:** Scientist/agronomist style illustration of IPM pyramid (prevention → monitoring → biological control → chemical as last resort), clean technical aesthetic
**CTA:** "Ver la guía — 19,90 €"
**Funnel:** Consideration (credibility / anti-hype)

**Why it works:** Farmers are tired of products that overclaim. This angle is honest — "we won't save your trees, but we'll help you understand and act better." That credibility is a differentiator.

**Compliance note:** "Science-based" requires citing sources. Mention "IFAPA, RAIF, estudios publicados" as credibility anchors.

## PART 6: META COMPLIANCE NOTES

**ALLOWED:**
- "Reduce el riesgo de daño"
- "Ayuda a detectar señales"
- "Manejo integrado basado en fuentes técnicas"
- "19,90 € — Acceso anticipado"
- "Consulta siempre con un técnico antes de aplicar tratamientos"

**FORBIDDEN:**
- ❌ "Elimina al 100%"
- ❌ "Resultados garantizados"
- ❌ "Salva todos tus árboles"
- ❌ "Solución definitiva"
- ❌ Before/after fake photos claiming cure
- ❌ Testimonials from fake farmers
