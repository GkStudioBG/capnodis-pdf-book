## 🎬 REEL 1 — AWARENESS
**Purpose:** Scroll-stop. Problem recognition.
**Target:** Farmers who notice trees drying but don't know why.
**Hook text:** "¿Tus almendros se secan sin motivo?"

### PROMPT — Reel 1

```
A single distressed almond tree stands in a sun-baked Spanish orchard. The ground is cracked and dry. The tree's leaves are yellowing from the bottom up, but the top still has some green leaves. Shot from eye-level, slowly push in toward the trunk base. Cut to a low-angle macro of the bark near the soil line — small entry holes and fine sawdust/frass visible. Fade to a dark background with golden text overlay.

Real footage style, not cartoon. Warm afternoon Mediterranean sun, dust particles in the air. Color palette: dry yellow-orange earth, dark green shadows, cream highlights. Cinematic, tense, honest. No music — just natural ambient sound of wind and cicadas.

CAMERA: slow push-in, slight Dutch angle at 10s mark, hold on final text card.
LENS: 35mm equivalent, shallow depth of field, background slightly blurred.
SCENE LENGTH: 10s real footage + 5s text card.

TEXT OVERLAYS (add in Capcut):
- 0:04 — "¿Se secan sin motivo?" (white, centered)
- 0:08 — "El daño empieza bajo tierra" (white, gold accent line below)
- 0:12 — "Gusano cabezudo — Guía práctica" (white)
- 0:14 — "19,90 €" (bold, gold color, dark green background)

NEGATIVE PROMPT: cartoon, animation, AI-looking face, bright green grass, heavy saturation, text spelling errors, blurry footage, shaky handheld.
```

**Target:** Farmers who know about Capnodis but don't understand the cycle.
**Hook text:** "No es sequía. Es lo que no ves."

### PROMPT — Reel 2

```
Underground cross-section view of an almond tree root system in dark soil. The roots are partially visible — healthy pale roots on the right, dark damaged roots with larval tunnels on the left. Animated glowing dots mark the damage zones. Camera tilts down slowly into the root system. Cut to: time-lapse style sequence of adult Capnodis beetles emerging from the soil (dark buprestid beetles, 2-3cm long) in spring light. Cut to: flat-lay of the PDF guide cover on a wooden table, pages slowly flipping, showing calendar grid and checklist lines. End on split: root damage diagram left, guide cover right.

Style: technical illustration meets documentary. Clean, educational, not sterile. Dark soil background (#1a1208), gold annotations (#cfb035), cream document backgrounds. Agricultural, precise, professional.

CAMERA: macro tilt-down into roots, smooth crossfade between scenes, slow zoom on guide pages.
LENS: macro lens for root details, medium shot for beetle emergence, overhead for document flat-lay.
SCENE LENGTH: 10s footage + 5s text card.

TEXT OVERLAYS (add in Capcut):
```
